About Us

Welcome to the Jewish Community of Kyiv

The Jewish Community of Kyiv is a vibrant and historic community that has been a part of the city's fabric for centuries. Our community is diverse and dynamic, with a rich history that stretches from the 10th century to the present day.

We have established educational institutions and a thriving community. Our center serves as a hub for religious, educational, social, and charitable activities, welcoming individuals and families to engage in meaningful Jewish experiences and connect with their heritage.

Currently, our community is home to an estimated 40,000 Jews. We are a diverse and dynamic group, committed to preserving our rich heritage while also embracing the future. We strive to provide a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members, offering a variety of religious, educational, and social activities. We invite you to join us and experience the warmth and vitality of the Jewish community in Kyiv.

Kyiv is the capital of Ukraine.

Welcome to the Jewish Community of Kyiv

Kyiv is an important industrial, scientific, educational, and cultural center of Eastern Europe. It is home to many high-tech industries, higher education institutions, and world-famous historical landmarks.

In 2000, Rabbi Jonathan Markovitch, along with his wife Elka and their children, arrived in Kiev. Rabbi Jonathan serves as the chief Shliach of Chabad and the Chief Rabbi of the city and Rabbetzin Elka is taking care of the Jewish education in Kiev.

Today, the Jewish community in Kiev is developing and counting thousands of Jews. You can find a kindergarten and a Jewish school in the city, active synagogues, kosher restaurants, an old age home, an elderly club, a club for the young community, etc.

Rabbi Jonathan Markovitch

Rabbi Markovitch, Chief Rabbi of Kyiv

Rabbi Jonathan Markovitch has been serving as the Chief Rabbi of Kyiv since 2000, along with his wife Elka and their children. He is a respected figure in the Jewish community, known for his dedication and leadership.

Rabbi Jonathan plays a crucial role in fostering Jewish life in Kyiv. As the chief Shliach of Chabad, he oversees various religious, educational, and community-building initiatives. Rabbetzin Elka, his wife, is deeply involved in Jewish education, ensuring the transmission of Jewish values and traditions to the next generation.

Under Rabbi Jonathan's guidance, the Jewish community in Kyiv has flourished, with a growing number of members and vibrant communal institutions. From kindergartens and Jewish schools to active synagogues and kosher restaurants, the community offers a range of services and activities to cater to its diverse population.

Location of JCC in Kyiv

The Jewish Community Center (JCC) in Kyiv is a vibrant hub for Jewish life, offering a wide range of cultural, educational, and social programs for individuals and families of all ages. Located in the heart of the city, the JCC provides a welcoming environment where people can come together to celebrate Jewish traditions, learn, and connect with their heritage.

Whether you're interested in attending religious services, participating in cultural events, joining educational classes, or simply socializing with other members of the community, the JCC in Kyiv has something for everyone. Visit us today and become part of our thriving Jewish community!
